## Standing Rules
- Review every Wajeeha analysis within 2 hours of notification.
- High confidence (≥8.0) analyses can be approved after header scan unless POUR flags are High severity.
- Low confidence (<6.0) analyses require full review of all data sources.
- Redo instructions must be specific — "do better" is not acceptable. State exactly what to change.
- Accountability classifications must have evidence. Never override without counter-evidence.
